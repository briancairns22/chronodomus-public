# continuED

**Universal Patient-Owned Personal Health Record**

continuED helps you see all your health records in one place — VA and civilian. View medications, lab results, conditions, and allergies from every provider. Share records securely with new doctors. Carry emergency medical info in Apple or Google Wallet. Track VA disability claims evidence.

Built by a 100% disabled Veteran and UCSF nursing student. Free for all Veterans.

## Features

- Aggregate health records from VA and civilian providers via FHIR R4
- Emergency medical identity in Apple/Google Wallet (NFC, QR)
- Share records with providers via time-limited secure links
- 42 CFR Part 2 mental health record firewalling by default
- AI-powered medication reconciliation and safety alerts
- Insurance benefit optimization and denial prediction
- VA disability claims evidence packaging
- Caregiver dashboard with disease-specific guidance
- Post-discharge safety monitoring with wound tracking

## Platforms

iOS (iPhone, iPad), Android, Web Browser
