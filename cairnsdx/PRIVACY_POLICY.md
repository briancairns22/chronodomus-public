# Privacy Policy

**CairnsDx — Lab Intelligence Platform**

**Last Updated: April 18, 2026**

---

Cairns Care LLC ("we," "us," "our") operates CairnsDx (cairnsdx.com). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our Service.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect:

- **(a) Account information** — name, email address, date of birth
- **(b) Health information you provide** — lab results, diagnoses, symptoms, medications you enter or upload
- **(c) Usage data** — pages visited, features used, upload history

We do **not** collect data from third-party tracking pixels, advertising networks, or social media SDKs on authenticated pages.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Data

Your health data is used exclusively to:

- Provide lab analysis and cross-domain interpretive reports
- Generate gap recommendations and clinical context
- Improve the CairnsDx algorithm under de-identified, aggregate analysis

We do **not** sell your data. We do **not** share your data with advertisers. We do **not** use your data for marketing to third parties.

---

## 3. DCLS Supervision

All interpretive outputs are supervised by a licensed Doctor of Clinical Laboratory Science (DCLS). Your data may be reviewed by our DCLS team for quality assurance and clinical accuracy.

---

## 4. Data Storage & Encryption

All personal health information (PHI) is:

- Encrypted at rest using AES-256
- Encrypted in transit using TLS 1.2+
- Stored in access-restricted, audit-logged databases
- Protected using deterministic encryption for queryable fields and standard encryption for all other PHI

---

## 5. No Third-Party Tracking on Health Pages

CairnsDx does not use Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, or any third-party JavaScript on authenticated pages where your health data is visible. Analytics are server-side only. This is a policy commitment, not just a technical choice.

---

## 6. Health Breach Notification

As a Personal Health Record (PHR) vendor under the FTC Health Breach Notification Rule (16 CFR Part 318), we will notify you within 60 days of discovering any unauthorized disclosure of your health information.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **(a)** Access all your data
- **(b)** Export your data in standard formats (JSON, PDF)
- **(c)** Correct inaccurate data
- **(d)** Delete your account and all associated data permanently
- **(e)** Revoke consent at any time

---

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days. De-identified aggregate data may be retained for algorithm improvement.

---

## 9. Children

CairnsDx is not intended for use by individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

---

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before they take effect.
